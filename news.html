Mac OS Big Sur has telemetry

Hey mac users! Apple has stuck telemetry into Mac OS Big Sur.

With software like "little snitch" being useless because of the measures Apple has stuck in MacOS Big Sur.

Not only did Little Snitch become useless but VPNs can no longer help either. As apple put measures in to prevent VPNs from stopping the data collection as well.

If you are scared about being spied on maybe try installing Windows or Linux on your macbook or iMac :D
